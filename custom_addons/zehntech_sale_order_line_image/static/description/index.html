Display Product Images in Sales Orders, Quotations, and Invoices

Product images are shown directly in order and invoice lines, making documents more visually clear. Customers can quickly recognize products, improving accuracy and reducing confusion.

Show Images in PDF reports for Sales Orders and Invoices

Images are included in generated PDF reports, ensuring professional, image-rich documents for sharing with customers. This adds visual clarity while maintaining standard Odoo layouts.

Keep Layouts Clean with Default Placeholders for Missing Images

If a product has no image, a blank space or placeholder is shown. This ensures the layout remains neat and avoids breaking the document structure.

Enable or Disable Image Display Easily from Sales Settings

Admins can turn image display enable or disable from Sales Settings with just a checkbox. This gives flexibility to businesses that may not always want product images in documents.

Support Images for Both Products and Product Variants

Images for both main products and their variants (e.g., colors, sizes) are displayed. This ensures that the exact chosen item is visually represented in sales documents.
